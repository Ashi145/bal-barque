Our Story

A passion for flavor, a commitment to quality

Welcome to BAL-BARBEQUE & BAKERY

Founded in 2015, BAL-BARBEQUE & BAKERY was born from a simple idea: what if you could enjoy world-class BBQ and artisan baked goods all under one roof?

Our founder, Chef Marcus BAL, spent years perfecting the art of slow-smoked meats in Texas before returning home to combine his two greatest passions - authentic BBQ and traditional European baking techniques.

Today, we're proud to serve our community with the same commitment to quality and flavor that started it all. Every rib is smoked low and slow, every loaf is baked fresh daily, and every dish is made with love.
